üìä Opportunity Score Explained

The Opportunity Score (0-100) indicates the priority for product improvement. Higher scores mean higher priority.

Score Calculation:

  • Negative Feedback Frequency (40%): Ratio of negative posts to total posts for the product
  • Recency (30%): Weight given to recent negative feedback (last 30 days)
  • Engagement Level (30%): Views, comments, and reactions indicate issue visibility

Example: A product with 80% negative posts, many from the last week, and high engagement = High Opportunity Score (80-100)
